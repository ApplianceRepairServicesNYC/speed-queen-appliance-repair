Commercial-Grade Speed Queen Front Load Washers

Speed Queen Front Load washers are built with commercial-grade components designed to last 25 years. These machines feature heavy-duty bearings, a robust suspension system, and the patented stainless steel wash tub that outperforms residential competitors. The front-loading design uses less water and detergent while delivering superior cleaning performance.

Speed Queen's Dynamic Balancing Technology ensures quiet operation even with unbalanced loads, while the high spin speeds extract more water to reduce drying time. These machines are built in Ripon, Wisconsin with American craftsmanship and backed by industry-leading warranties.

Features and Advantages

Speed Queen Front Load washers deliver commercial performance for residential use.

  • Commercial-grade construction built to last 25 years

Common Problems and Professional Solutions

Washer Not Draining

Drainage issues often stem from clogged drain pumps, kinked hoses, or debris in the drain filter. Our technicians clear obstructions and restore proper drainage quickly.

Excessive Vibration

Vibration problems may indicate worn shock absorbers, damaged suspension springs, or an unbalanced drum. We diagnose and repair suspension components for smooth, quiet operation.

Door Seal Leaks

Worn or damaged door boot seals allow water to leak during cycles. We replace door gaskets with genuine Speed Queen parts to eliminate leaks.

Motor or Drive Issues

Motor problems manifest as the drum not spinning, slow spin speeds, or unusual noises. Our technicians diagnose motor and drive belt issues and perform expert repairs.

Legendary Speed Queen Top Load Washers

Speed Queen Top Load washers are the gold standard in laundry equipment, featuring the classic agitator design that delivers thorough cleaning without the complexity of front loaders. Built with commercial-grade components including a heavy-duty transmission and stainless steel tub, these machines are engineered to last 25 years in residential use.

The traditional top-loading design offers easy loading without bending, faster cycle times, and the ability to add items mid-cycle. Speed Queen's agitator technology provides powerful cleaning action that removes tough stains while being gentle on fabrics.

Key Features and Benefits

Speed Queen Top Load washers combine traditional design with modern durability.

  • Commercial-grade transmission built to last
  • Stainless steel wash tub

Common Problems and Professional Solutions

Agitator Not Working

Agitator problems may indicate worn agitator dogs, transmission issues, or drive belt failures. Our technicians diagnose and repair agitator mechanisms for proper wash action.

Washer Won't Spin

Spin cycle failures often result from lid switch problems, motor coupling failures, or transmission issues. We identify the cause and restore proper spin function.

Water Not Filling

Fill problems may stem from faulty inlet valves, water level sensors, or control board issues. Our technicians repair fill system components for reliable operation.

Transmission Problems

Transmission failures cause grinding noises, failure to agitate, or spin issues. We repair or replace transmissions with genuine Speed Queen parts.

Speed Queen dryers are built with the same commercial-grade components used in laundromat machines, designed to deliver consistent performance for 25 years. Available in both electric and gas configurations, these dryers feature large-diameter drums, commercial-grade bearings, and durable galvanized steel cabinets.

Speed Queen's dryers offer impressive cycle times and thorough drying, thanks to optimized airflow patterns and precise temperature control. The reversible door option provides installation flexibility, while the large door opening makes loading and unloading easy.

Speed Queen dryers deliver fast, thorough drying with commercial reliability.

  • Four temperature settings

Common Problems and Professional Solutions

Dryer Not Heating

Electric dryers may have failed heating elements, thermal fuses, or thermostats. Gas dryers may have igniter, gas valve, or flame sensor issues. Our technicians diagnose and repair all heating system components.

Long Drying Times

Extended dry times often indicate restricted airflow from clogged lint screens, blocked vents, or failing blower wheels. We restore proper airflow for efficient drying.

Drum Not Turning

Belt, motor, or idler pulley failures can prevent drum rotation. Our technicians diagnose drive system issues and restore proper tumbling action.

Unusual Noises

Squealing, thumping, or grinding noises may indicate worn drum rollers, damaged belts, or failing bearings. We replace worn components for quiet operation.
